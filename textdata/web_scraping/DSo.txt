DSo

Achaemenid Royal Inscriptions: collection of Old Persian cuneiform texts from the sixth, fifth, and fourth centuries BCE, left by the Achaemenid kings on their official monuments.DSo, inscription on a slab of marble from Susa
Inscription in Old Persian and Babylonian, written on a slab of marble.

[lacuna] naša
tam \ akunavam \ thâtiy \ Dâ
rayavauš \ XŠ \ vašnâ \ AMha \ Çûš
âyâ \ idâ \ frašam \ akunavam


[...] this relief I made.
Darius the King says: by the grace of Ahuramazda, I built something excellent here at Susa.

Literature

Pierre Lecoq, Les inscriptions de la Perse achéménide (1997 Paris)
